About us

There always was the lack of innovations and simplicity in logistics. 2017 year became a period of problem detection and invention the tool to solve it. We create a platform that brings together all the members of transport sector in a particular communication field. Our system is capable to combine a demand and supply in almost a real time, also offering a maximal efficiency in customer cooperation with the contractors. A large number of actors such as a dispatcher, loader, cargo owner and others form an overcomplicated system that regularly suffers from a human factor. We reduce the probability of error by eliminating the most part of manual work and providing a real-time communication. Once something that was called an idea turned into a perspective IT service provider that is focused on the European market ensuring the appropriate level of attendance.

Problems

Cargo owner

-Lack of confidence in the safety of cargo transportation

-The high cost of transportation services

-Delivery delay

Freight forwarder

-Spending too much time by searching the optimal candidate to release the transportation

-Office inefficiency: insufficient control over the execution of orders in real time

-Complicated communication system between all participants involved in process

Transportation company

-Empty mileage of the truck

-Underloading the transport

-Wasting a value time on loading/unloading a car

-Too long downtime for truck, too much time spent by searching for an order

-The problem of communication with the customer and freight forwarder

Warehouse

-Office inefficiency: lack of control over execution of the order, disorganization

-Outdated and ineffective communication methods with customers and other contract participants

Solutions

  • Cargo owner
  • Freight forwarder
  • Transportation company
  • Warehouse

Cargo owner

-Careful identity checking of the carrier

-Reducing the cost of transportation services with the same or even higher margin of the contractor

-Regular informing the customer about driver's key indicators. In the case of non-compliance with the regulations, the contract with the carrier will be canceled.

Freight forwarder

-Speeding up the searching of the optimal carrier

-Ensuring the control over the execution of an order

-Possibility of direct anonymous communication between the parties of the contract

Transportation company

-The program provides orders, so it saves time on their surching and reduces the annual truck downtime

-Reducing the empty mileage of the truck due to maximal upload

-The system will automatically inform the potential customers about possibility of uploading the truck, showing its location

-The driver will receive the information about time and place of cooperation with the warehouse

Warehouse

-Control over the quality of provided services by photoreports of uploading and unloading the transport

-The program provides users with checklists and notifications system that constantly organizes and keeps up to date all the participants of the contract

-Ability to choose a communication template and guaranteed anonymity
