About us

There always was the lack of innovations and simplicity in logistics. 2017 year became a period of problem detection and invention the tool to solve it. We create a platform that brings together all the members of transport sector in a particular communication field. Our system is capable to combine a demand and supply in almost a real time, also offering a maximal efficiency in customer cooperation with the contractors. A large number of actors such as a dispatcher, loader, cargo owner and others form an overcomplicated system that regularly suffers from a human factor. We reduce the probability of error by eliminating the most part of manual work and providing a real-time communication. Once something that was called an idea turned into a perspective IT service provider that is focused on the European market ensuring the appropriate level of attendance.
